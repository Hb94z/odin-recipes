Morning eggs

This is a simple scrambled egg recipe including some avocado

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • Lemon juice
  • Bread
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Break 3 eggs into a saucepan and mix well
  2. Cut open your avocado, put contents into a bowl
  3. Add salt, pepper and lemon juice, then mix well till soft
  5. Cook the eggs well, taking off heat regularly while constantly stiring
  6. When eggs reach desired consistency remove from heat
  7. Serve with bread of choice
