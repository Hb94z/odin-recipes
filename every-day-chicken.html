Every Day Chicken

This is a typical easy every day meal I like to eat. It is high in protein, simple to make and tastes good.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken thigh cut or uncut
  • Salad of choice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Small amount of cooking oil
  • Egg plant
  • Rice

Method

  1. Cook some rice however you prefer so it is ready when you finish the following steps
  2. Put all chicken thigh into a pan, cover in a small amount of cooking oil, then proceed to salt and pepper to your liking
  3. Heat the pan and allow to cook gently
  4. At this time prepare your salad
  5. Stir the chicken regularly
  6. Cut one egg plant into large cubed slices to prepare it for frying
  7. When chicken is ready, remove from the pan and immediately add the egg plant to the hot pan
  8. Stir and add salt
  9. When slightly browned, take off heat and serve
